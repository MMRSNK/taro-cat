Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the ТАРО tarot bot (the "Bot", operating the Threads account @myaufar) handles information. The Bot is provided for entertainment purposes only.

What the Bot does

The Bot posts daily tarot forecasts on Threads and replies to public posts that mention @myaufar with a personalized tarot reading (an image and text).

Information we process

  • Public mention content — when you publicly mention the Bot, we read the text of your post (your question) and your public username in order to generate and post a reply. This data is already public on Threads.
  • Mention identifiers — we store the Threads ID of each mention we have already answered, so the Bot does not reply to the same post twice. We do not store the text of your messages after a reply is generated.

We do not collect emails, passwords, contact lists, location, or any sensitive personal data. We do not sell or share your data with advertisers.

Third-party services

  • Meta / Threads API — to read public mentions and publish posts and replies.
  • OpenAI — your question text is sent to OpenAI to generate the tarot reading text. See OpenAI's privacy policy at openai.com/policies/privacy-policy.
  • Image hosting (catbox.moe) — the generated reading image is uploaded so it can be attached to the Threads post.

Data retention

Only mention IDs are retained (to prevent duplicate replies). You can request their removal at any time — see Data Deletion.

Children

The Bot is not intended for children. Tarot content is for adults and is entertainment only.

Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: 27 June 2026

The ТАРО tarot bot (@myaufar) stores very little data: only the Threads IDs of mentions it has already replied to, so it does not answer the same post twice. It does not store the text of your messages or any profile data.

How to request deletion

To have any stored mention identifiers associated with your account removed, email mamros.k@gmail.com with the subject "Data deletion" and include your Threads username.

We will delete the relevant records within 30 days and confirm by email.

Note

Any public replies the Bot posted on Threads can also be removed by reporting or replying to the post; deletion of public Threads content is ultimately managed through Threads itself.
